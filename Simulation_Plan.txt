Plan for Utilizing D WAVE Sentient World Simulations in Policy Reform and Strategy Development for a New World Order:

1. Identify key policy areas for reform and strategy development
2. Develop a comprehensive understanding of the capabilities and limitations of D WAVE Sentient World Simulations
3. Determine the ethical and legal considerations of the project
4. Develop a simulation framework that takes into account the identified policy areas, capabilities and limitations of D WAVE Sentient World Simulations, and ethical and legal considerations
5. Run simulations and analyze results
6. Develop policy reform and strategies based on simulation results
7. Implement policy reform and strategies
8. Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of policy reform and strategies
